Resources

Resources for individuals currently experiancing homelessness in Austin

Click on each one to find out more about the services they offer and their location

Austin Resource Center for the Homeless

This facility is owned by the City of Austin and operated by Front Steps

Austin Shelter for Women and Children

This facility is owned by Travis county and operated by the Salvation Army

Caritas of Austin

Caritas of Austin provides housing, food, educational, career, and other supportive services.

Mobile Loaves and Fishes

Mobile Loaves and Fishes provides food, clothing, hygiene products, and housing

The SAFE

The SAFE supports survivors of child abuse, sexual assault and exploitation, and domestic violence.

National Alliance on Mental Illness Austin

National Alliance on Mental Illness Austin offers services for people affected by mental illness.
