United Way for Greater Austin

United Way for Greater Austin Volunteers strengthens communities through meaningful volunteer action and strives to inspire, equip and mobilize people to change lives through service. We engage thousands of Central Texans in volunteerism annually and empower people to make volunteering a regular part of their lives.United Way ATX Volunteers is a trusted partner, aligning volunteer human capital, financial resources, in-kind donations and project management expertise with the needs of our community. They have organized volunteer projects benefiting our local parks and trails, nonprofit agencies, schools, and community centers.

Volunteering Can Include...

  • Assemble kits to support immediate need services
  • Hang out with teens
  • Working on local parks and trails
  • Working in community centers
