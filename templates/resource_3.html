Caritas of Austin Caritas of Austin provides housing, food, educational, career, and other supportive services.

What is Caritas of Austin?

Caritas of Austin provides many essetial services to assist those currently in homelessness. Stable housing is where our work to end homelessness begins.Food services ensure people have healthy food as they rebuild their lives. Education classes teach life skills to help people reach their full potential. Job placement and career development build a strong foundation for families.

Services Include:

  • Housing
  • Education
  • Food
  • Employment
  • Veterans Assitance
